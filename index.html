Homepage of Mitchell J Sullivan


Bioinformatician and software developer

I am PhD candidate at the University of Queensland in the Beatson microbial genomics lab.

During my undergraduate studies I worked as a research assistant in the Beatson lab developing protocols for the assembly of genomes for collaborators of the group. I stayed with the Beatson group to undertake postgraduate studies, researching phage-mediated lateral genetic transfer in E. coli. My doctoral thesis focuses on developing new ways of visualising sequencing data and assemblies.

In my spare time I enjoy listening, playing and composing music.
